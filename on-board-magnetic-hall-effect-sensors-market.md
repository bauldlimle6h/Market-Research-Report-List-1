Earth Field Sensors, ranging from 1 microgauss to 10 gauss, detect the Earth's natural magnetic field, useful for navigation systems. Above 10 Gauss, Bias Magnetic Field Sensors are designed for environments with stronger magnetic fields, commonly found in industrial and automotive applications, ensuring reliable signal processing in various conditions.</p></p>
