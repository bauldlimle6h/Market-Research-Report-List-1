<p><p>The papaya seed oil market is primarily divided into two extraction methods: chemical extraction and physical squeeze. Chemical extraction involves the use of solvents to efficiently draw out oil from papaya seeds, often resulting in a higher yield but potentially introducing residual chemicals. In contrast, physical squeeze, or cold pressing, utilizes mechanical pressure to extract oil without chemicals, preserving the oil's natural properties and nutrients. Each method caters to different market preferences, influencing quality, pricing, and consumer demand.</p></p>
