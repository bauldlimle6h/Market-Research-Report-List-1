<p><p>The biomedical adhesives and sealants market is divided into two main types: biomedical adhesives and biomedical sealants. Biomedical adhesives are substances used to bond tissues and devices within the body, facilitating wound closure and surgical procedures. They offer advantages like minimal invasiveness and improved healing. Biomedical sealants, on the other hand, are primarily used to seal tissue surfaces, preventing leakage and enhancing tissue integrity. Both types play crucial roles in various medical applications, including surgeries, wound care, and tissue engineering.</p></p>
