<p><p>Microchannel Plate Detectors (MCPs) are categorized into three primary types based on their configuration: Single, Chevron, and Z-Stack. Single MCPs consist of a single layer of microchannels, providing basic detection capabilities. Chevron MCPs, featuring two stacked plates arranged in a V-shape, enhance gain and spatial resolution. Z-Stack MCPs utilize multiple layers in a vertical arrangement to maximize detection efficiency and minimize noise. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to various applications in fields like imaging and spectroscopy.</p></p>
