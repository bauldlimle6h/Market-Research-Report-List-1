<p><p>The medical screen protectors market encompasses three primary types: PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane), and other materials. PET protectors are known for their rigidity and scratch resistance, making them suitable for high-traffic medical environments. TPU protectors, on the other hand, offer flexibility, durability, and excellent adhesion, which helps in absorbing shocks and impacts. The "Other" category includes specialized materials offering unique benefits like antimicrobial properties or enhanced clarity, catering to specific medical applications and environments.</p></p>
