<p><p>The cataract treatment devices market encompasses several key types, including Intraocular Lenses (IOLs), Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Devices (OVDs), and phacoemulsification equipment. IOLs are artificial lenses implanted to restore vision post-cataract surgery. OVDs are gel-like substances used to maintain eye shape and protect tissues during surgery. Phacoemulsification equipment employs ultrasound technology to break up cataracts for easier removal. Together, these devices play a crucial role in enhancing the safety and effectiveness of cataract surgical procedures.</p></p>
