<p><p>Antiviral combination therapies enhance treatment efficacy by using multiple drug classes to combat viral infections. The NRTI/NNRTI category combines nucleoside and non-nucleoside reverse transcriptase inhibitors to impede viral replication. Integrase Inhibitor/NRTI pairs utilize integrase inhibitors with NRTIs to block viral DNA integration into the host genome. NRTI/Protease Inhibitor combinations target different stages of the viral lifecycle, enhancing effectiveness. The "Others" category includes innovative combinations that may involve novel mechanisms or drug classes tailored to specific viral challenges.</p></p>
