<p><strong>The Endoscopic Balloon Dilator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Lumen</li><li>Double Lumen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endoscopic balloon dilator market consists of two main types: single lumen and double lumen. Single lumen dilators feature a solitary channel, allowing for straightforward inflation to treat strictures in the esophagus and other organs. In contrast, double lumen dilators possess two channels, enabling simultaneous functions such as inflation and drainage. This design enhances procedural efficiency and flexibility during endoscopic interventions. The choice between single and double lumen dilators often depends on specific medical requirements and the complexity of the conditions being treated.</p></p>
