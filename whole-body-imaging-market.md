<p><p>The whole-body imaging market encompasses various imaging modalities, primarily including Computed Tomography (CT) scans and Positron Emission Tomography (PET) scans. CT scans utilize X-rays to produce detailed cross-sectional images of the body, aiding in diagnosing various conditions, including tumors and internal injuries. PET scans, on the other hand, utilize radioactive tracers to visualize metabolic activity, making them valuable for oncology and neurology applications. Together, these modalities enhance diagnostic accuracy and treatment planning, driving their increasing adoption in healthcare.</p></p>
