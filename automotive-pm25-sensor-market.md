<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive PM2.5 sensor market is categorized into single-channel and dual-channel types based on their measurement capabilities. Single-channel sensors detect particulate matter using a single measurement path, offering a cost-effective solution for basic air quality monitoring. In contrast, dual-channel sensors utilize two measurement paths, allowing for enhanced accuracy and reliability by comparing data from different sources. This results in improved detection of particulate matter concentration, making dual-channel sensors suitable for advanced applications in modern vehicles focused on comprehensive air quality management.</p></p>
