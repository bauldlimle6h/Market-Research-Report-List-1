<p><strong>The Car Braking Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Box System</li><li>One-Box System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car braking device market can be categorized into Two-Box and One-Box systems. The Two-Box system separates the brake components into two distinct modules, offering enhanced performance and easier maintenance. This design allows for better heat dissipation and improved safety features. In contrast, the One-Box system integrates the braking components into a single unit, providing a compact and lightweight solution. This system is often cost-effective and easier to install, making it popular in modern vehicle designs, particularly for electric and hybrid models.</p></p>
