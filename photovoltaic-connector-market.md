<p><strong>The Photovoltaic Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC Photovoltaic Connector</li><li>DC Photovoltaic Connector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic connector market is primarily divided into two types: AC and DC photovoltaic connectors. AC photovoltaic connectors are designed to handle alternating current, facilitating the connection of solar panels to inverters and grid systems, ensuring efficient energy transmission. On the other hand, DC photovoltaic connectors manage direct current, linking solar panels to batteries and other components in a solar power system. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing the performance and reliability of photovoltaic installations.</p></p>
