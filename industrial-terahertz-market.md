<p><strong>The Industrial Terahertz Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging</li><li>Active Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Terahertz market comprises two primary types: passive terahertz imaging and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging relies on detecting natural terahertz radiation emitted by objects, making it suitable for non-invasive inspections like quality control in manufacturing. In contrast, active terahertz imaging involves generating terahertz waves and analyzing their interactions with materials, enabling detailed analysis of physical properties, material characterization, and security applications. Both types offer unique advantages for various industrial sectors, enhancing inspection and diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
