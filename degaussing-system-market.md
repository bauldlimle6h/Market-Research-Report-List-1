<p><p>The degaussing system market is categorized into two primary types: external degaussing systems and shipborne degaussing systems. In contrast, shipborne degaussing systems are integrated directly into ships, providing real-time magnetic signature management to enhance stealth and reduce vulnerability to naval mines and torpedoes. Both systems play critical roles in naval operations by enhancing maritime security and ensuring operational effectiveness.</p></p>
