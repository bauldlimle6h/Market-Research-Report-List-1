<p><p>The Moisture Transmission Film market includes two primary types: Thermoplastic Polyolefins (TPOs) and Polyolefin Elastomers (POEs). TPOs are known for their excellent moisture barrier properties, lightweight nature, and thermal stability, making them suitable for applications in packaging and automotive sectors. In contrast, POEs offer superior flexibility and elasticity while maintaining moisture resistance. This combination enhances their usability in diverse applications, including medical packaging, food handling, and industrial uses, driving demand for moisture control solutions across various industries.</p></p>
