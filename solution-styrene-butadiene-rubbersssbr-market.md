<p><p>Solution Styrene Butadiene Rubbers (SSBR) are synthetic elastomers used in various applications, primarily in tires and automotive products. The SSBR market is categorized into two types: oil-filled SSBR and non-oil filled SSBR. Oil-filled SSBR incorporates processing oils to enhance flexibility and reduce production costs, improving performance in high-stress conditions. In contrast, non-oil filled SSBR offers superior mechanical properties and lower rolling resistance, making it ideal for high-performance tires. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
