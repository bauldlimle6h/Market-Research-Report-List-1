<p><p>The hybrid image sensor market is categorized into two main types: linear image sensors and area image sensors. Linear image sensors capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for applications like document scanners and barcode scanners, providing high resolution in narrow formats. In contrast, area image sensors capture entire images at once, making them suitable for cameras and smartphones, delivering comprehensive visuals. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, from security to consumer electronics, enhancing image capture capabilities.</p></p>
