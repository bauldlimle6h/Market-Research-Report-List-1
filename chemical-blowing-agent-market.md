<p><p>The chemical blowing agent market is categorized into several types: Hydrochlorofluorocarbons (HCFCs), Hydrocarbons (HCs), Hydrofluorocarbons (HFCs), and others. HCFCs were historically used for their effectiveness but are being phased out due to environmental concerns. HCs, including propane and butane, are gaining popularity for being more eco-friendly. HFCs are alternatives to HCFCs but still face scrutiny due to their global warming potential. Other agents include non-chemical methods and bio-based agents, addressing sustainability and regulatory challenges in foam production.</p></p>
