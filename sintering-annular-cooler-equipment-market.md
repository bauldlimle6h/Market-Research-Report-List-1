<p><ul><li>Transmission Device</li><li>Rotary Device</li><li>Feed & Discharge Hopper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sintering annular cooler equipment market can be categorized into three main types: transmission devices, rotary devices, and feed & discharge hoppers. Transmission devices ensure the smooth transfer of materials within the cooling process, enhancing efficiency. Rotary devices facilitate the rotation of the sintered material, promoting even cooling and optimal performance. Feed and discharge hoppers manage the input and output of materials, maintaining a continuous workflow during the sintering process, thus impacting overall productivity and operational effectiveness.</p></p>
