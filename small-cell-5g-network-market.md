<p><strong>The Small Cell 5G Network Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Picocells</li><li>Femtocells</li><li>Microcells</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Small cell 5G networks encompass three primary types: picocells, femtocells, and microcells. Picocells are compact, low-power base stations designed for indoor use, enhancing coverage in specific areas like malls or stadiums. Femtocells serve residential or small business environments, providing localized coverage through user-installed devices. Microcells are slightly larger and typically deployed outdoors, improving coverage in urban environments or high-density areas. Together, these small cells address coverage gaps and enhance network capacity, supporting the growing demand for mobile data.</p></p>
