<p><ul><li>Light</li><li>Heavy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The renewable naphtha market is divided into two main types: light and heavy. Light renewable naphtha is characterized by lower boiling points and higher quality, making it suitable for blending into gasoline and petrochemical feedstocks. Heavy renewable naphtha, on the other hand, has higher boiling points and is primarily used in the production of more complex hydrocarbons or as a feedstock for refining processes. Both types play a crucial role in the transition to sustainable energy, catering to different industrial needs.</p></p>
