<p><p>The hybrid polymer capacitors market includes two primary types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and radial lead types. SMD capacitors are designed for modern electronic circuits, offering compact sizes and efficient surface mounting, which enhances automated assembly processes. Radial lead types have leads extending from one end, making them suitable for through-hole mounting in various applications. Both types deliver high performance, improved thermal stability, and longer life cycles, making them ideal for consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications.</p></p>
