Jet injectors use high-pressure air to deliver insulin through the skin without needles, offering a needle-free alternative. Insulin pumps continuously deliver insulin and can be programmed for individualized therapy, making them suitable for intensive management of diabetes. Each device caters to varying patient preferences and needs in diabetes care.</p></p>
