<p><strong>The Foam Roller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hollow Foam Roller</li><li>Solid Foam Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foam roller market is primarily categorized into two types: hollow foam rollers and solid foam rollers. Hollow foam rollers are lightweight and often feature a tubular design, making them suitable for travel and easy storage. They generally provide moderate firmness and are preferred for various stretching and rehabilitation exercises. In contrast, solid foam rollers offer a denser structure, delivering more intense pressure for deep tissue massage and muscle recovery, making them ideal for athletes and those seeking targeted relief.</p></p>
