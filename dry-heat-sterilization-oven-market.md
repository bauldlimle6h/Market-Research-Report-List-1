<p><p>The Dry Heat Sterilization Oven market is divided into two primary types: Continuous Dry Heat Sterilization and Intermittent Dry Heat Sterilization. Continuous systems operate non-stop, providing a steady sterilization process suitable for high-volume production environments, ensuring consistent microbial eradication. In contrast, Intermittent Dry Heat Sterilization involves periodic cycles, making it ideal for smaller batches or specific applications, allowing flexibility and energy efficiency. Both methods effectively eliminate pathogens while preserving the integrity of heat-sensitive materials.</p></p>
