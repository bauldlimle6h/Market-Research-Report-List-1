<p><strong>The Composite Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic</li><li>Thermosetting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite resins market is primarily divided into thermoplastic and thermosetting types. Thermoplastic resins can be remolded and recycled, offering flexibility and durability, making them ideal for applications requiring repeated manufacturing processes. Conversely, thermosetting resins harden permanently when cured, providing excellent thermal and chemical resistance. They are often used in applications demanding high structural integrity, such as aerospace and automotive industries. This division allows various industries to choose the most suitable materials based on their specific needs.</p></p>
