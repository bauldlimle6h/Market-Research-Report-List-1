<p><p>The syrup glass bottles market features two primary types: low borosilicate glass and soda lime glass. Low borosilicate glass is known for its high thermal resistance and reduced expansion, making it suitable for pharmaceutical and high-quality syrup packaging. In contrast, soda lime glass is more commonly used due to its cost-effectiveness and adequate strength for general syrup storage. Both types offer excellent protection against environmental factors, ensuring syrup quality and longevity while serving varying market demands and applications.</p></p>
