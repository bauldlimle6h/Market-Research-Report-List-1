<p><ul><li>Hydraulic-Mechanical Transmission</li><li>Hydraulic Transmission</li><li>Power Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The towed belt loader market is characterized by three main types: Hydraulic-Mechanical Transmission, Hydraulic Transmission, and Power Transmission. Hydraulic-Mechanical Transmission combines hydraulic and mechanical systems for efficient movement and control, providing versatility in operation. Hydraulic Transmission relies solely on hydraulic systems for power transfer, ensuring smooth and powerful operation with minimal manual intervention. Power Transmission utilizes various drive mechanisms to transmit energy, often focusing on high efficiency and reliability in loading tasks. Each type serves distinct operational needs in airport logistics and material handling.</p></p>
