<p><p>The Standard IV Administration Sets market is segmented into vented and non-vented types. Vented IV administration sets have a built-in air vent that allows air to enter the fluid bag, ensuring smooth fluid flow and reducing the risk of air embolism. Non-vented IV sets, on the other hand, are designed for use with collapsible bags, eliminating the need for air entry, thus providing a more controlled delivery of medication. Both types are essential in ensuring safe and efficient intravenous therapy.</p></p>
