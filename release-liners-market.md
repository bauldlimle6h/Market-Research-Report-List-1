<p><ul><li>Release Linear Paper</li><li>Release Linear Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The release liners market consists of two primary types: release liner paper and release liner film. Release liner paper is typically made from specialized paper substrates that offer excellent release properties for adhesives and coatings, often used in medical and pressure-sensitive applications. In contrast, release liner film is crafted from plastic materials like polyethylene or polypropylene, providing enhanced durability and resistance to moisture. Both types serve vital roles in various industries, including packaging, construction, and automotive, facilitating easy handling and application of sticky products.</p></p>
