<p><strong>The Automatic Keratometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Double Image Method</li><li>Variable Double Image Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Keratometer market comprises two primary types: Fixed Double Image Method and Variable Double Image Method. The Fixed Double Image Method utilizes a stationary setup to measure corneal curvature by comparing two images of reflected light. In contrast, the Variable Double Image Method adjusts the distance between the images to obtain measurements. Both techniques facilitate precise assessments of corneal geometry, essential for diagnosing vision problems and planning refractive surgeries, catering to the evolving needs of the ophthalmology sector.</p></p>
