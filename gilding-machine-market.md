<p><p>The gilding machine market comprises various types, including round flattened and platen flattened gilding machines. Round flattened gilding machines are designed for applying gold leaf or foil to curved surfaces, ensuring even coverage on cylindrical objects. In contrast, platen flattened gilding machines utilize a flat surface to press and adhere gold onto flat materials, providing precise and consistent finishes. Both types cater to different applications in industries such as printing, packaging, and bookbinding, enhancing the aesthetic appeal of products.</p></p>
