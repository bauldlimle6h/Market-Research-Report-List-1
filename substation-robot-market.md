<p><p>The substation robot market is primarily categorized into two types: wheeled and crawler robots. Wheeled robots are designed for smooth, flat surfaces, making them suitable for navigating indoor substations with established pathways. They offer higher speed and efficiency in tasks like inspection and maintenance. In contrast, crawler robots feature tracks, allowing them to traverse uneven terrains and obstacles, which is advantageous in outdoor or rugged environments. Both types enhance operational safety and efficiency in substation management.</p></p>
