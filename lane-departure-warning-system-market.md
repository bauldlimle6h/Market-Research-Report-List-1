<p><ul><li>Lane Departure Warning (LWD)</li><li>Lane Keeping System (LKS)</li><li>Lane Centering Assist (LCA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lane departure warning system market comprises several key types: Lane Departure Warning (LDW) alerts drivers when they unintentionally drift from their lane, enhancing awareness and safety. Lane Keeping System (LKS) actively helps maintain lane position by providing steering assistance if the vehicle veers off course. Lane Centering Assist (LCA) further refines this by automatically adjusting steering to keep the vehicle centered within its lane. Together, these technologies improve driving safety by preventing unintended lane departures and enhancing overall vehicle control.</p></p>
