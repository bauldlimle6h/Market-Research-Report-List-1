<p><p>The ophthalmic laser devices market includes various types, each serving specific purposes in eye treatments. Diode lasers are used primarily for photocoagulation and retinal procedures. Femtosecond lasers enable precision in cataract surgery and corneal treatments. Excimer lasers are typically utilized for vision correction surgeries like LASIK. Nd:YAG lasers treat posterior capsule opacification and other conditions. Argon lasers are effective for retinal disorders, while SLT lasers are employed in glaucoma management by reducing intraocular pressure.</p></p>
