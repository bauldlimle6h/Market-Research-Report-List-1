<p><p>The cranial fixation stabilization system market comprises two primary types: cranial fixation systems and cranial stabilization systems. Cranial fixation systems are designed to securely hold the skull in place during surgical procedures, often utilizing plates and screws for stable support. In contrast, cranial stabilization systems focus on maintaining the positioning of the head and neck during surgery, providing support through various adjustable devices. Both systems are essential in neurosurgical and craniofacial surgeries, enhancing precision and patient safety.</p></p>
